Privacy Policy – KnitStitch

Last updated: May 13, 2025

KnitStitch ("the app") is a stitch counting tool developed by Deimante Studio. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

KnitStitch does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline on your device. No account is required. No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from users of any age.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at the email address listed on our Google Play store page.
